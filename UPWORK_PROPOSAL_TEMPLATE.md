## 4) Discovery questions to ask clients

### Business questions
1. What sales motion is this for: inbound, outbound, account-based, or mixed?
2. What does a “qualified lead” mean in your business today?
3. What is the cost of your team doing this manually right now?
4. What KPI matters most: speed, meeting rate, reply rate, conversion quality, or rep efficiency?
5. What happens after a lead is qualified?

### Process questions
1. Where do leads come from today?
2. What systems are involved: CRM, enrichment, email, spreadsheets, internal DB?
3. Who currently reviews or approves outreach?
4. Do you need full automation or human-in-the-loop review?
5. What edge cases usually break your current workflow?

### Technical questions
1. Which integrations matter most: Salesforce, HubSpot, Gmail, Outlook, Apollo, Clay, Slack, webhooks?
2. Do you want structured outputs for downstream automation?
3. How important are logs, observability, and retry logic?
4. Are there compliance or data-handling constraints?
5. Is this a prototype, an internal tool, or a production deployment?

### Scoping questions
1. How many leads per day or month should the system handle?
2. How personalized should the outreach be?
3. Do you already have scoring rules, or should I help define them?
4. What timeline matters most: speed to demo or long-term maintainability?
5. What budget range are you targeting?
